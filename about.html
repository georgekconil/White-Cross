The White cross of Colachel, at the altar of Thirusiluvai Nather Prayer Centre is a spiritual power source to eradicate the evil powers from the mind, body and life of many people who stream for relief, deliverence and attaining various blessings. The White Cross of Colachel is a miraculous cross full of life and and spirit. The blissful experiences from this cross attract many people and those who reach there experience the heavenly blessings and joy. Many feel pulse beat from the cross which which shows that Jesus is alive in it. Often many pilgrims physically feel pulling force from the cross that drag them towards it. When they catch hold ofthe cross with a prayerful mind they recieve heavenly graceand spiritual gifts which help them live happily overcoming worldly chores. The soil and small pebbles of Calvary are at the bottom of the cross. The powerand redemption of Calvary cross flows through this White Crosswhich helpmany to feel consolation in their life.

A holy Statue of Jesus depicted in Mark 15:17 -19, Mathew 27 :28-29 and John 19 : 1-5 is in front of the white cross which forms a centre of attraction as the pilgrims experience celestial message from the face of this holy statue , that is named in Tamil "Thirusiluvai Nather" - Lord of the Holy Cross, Keralites affectionately call him "Colachel Nathan". Lord Jesus beckons you too from the Whit Cross of Colachel.

Visionaries witness the power of Lord Jesus that flows around the world from the altar of the White Cross of Colachel shrine ,which purify and prepare the world for the Era of Peace -The Kingdom of God in this world, where the will of the Heavenly Father will be done on earth as it is done in heaven, where the whole humanity will come to know Jesus and have the real relationship with him. Everybody would enjoy full contentment of life where Jesus alone shall be the king and God. There shall be one shepherd and one flock . No evil powers shall prevail in this world for they will be eradicated completely from the world.

We feel the mighty power and presence of the Holy Trinity in the White Cross, who is capable of accomplishing the era of peace in this world. Anything is possible for Jesus. Amen!
